## 1. Purpose of this document

This document is the first populated example of the greenhouse knowledge capture template.

It uses the information already provided by the domain expert about the proposed greenhouse near Constanta, Romania.

Its purpose is:

- to convert raw expert narrative into a structured capture format,
- to preserve the existing level of detail,
- to identify what is already known,
- and to expose what is still missing before building a canonical digital greenhouse blueprint for Agro-DO.

### 6.2 Description in expert terms
The proposed greenhouse is a medium-size commercial greenhouse on a 5-hectare site near Constanta, Romania. It is a plastic-covered, medium-tech gothic structure made of 36 tunnels, each 10 meters wide and 120 meters long. The structure is intentionally reinforced because snow accumulation in winter is considered a serious risk. Pillar spacing is denser than common standard practice in order to improve structural resistance. The greenhouse includes zenith butterfly vents with motorized actuation and longitudinal gutters designed to collect rainwater and manage runoff through PVC downpipes and underground drainage lines.

- Structural material: TBD
- Main pillar profiles: Rectangular profile 120 x 80 mm in the front
- Main span logic: Gothic tunnel structure
- Front pillar spacing: One front pillar every 10 meters

- Internal pillar spacing (longitudinal): Every 2.5 meters along the tunnel
- Arches / gothic shape description: Gothic arch, ending at a peak rather than a full semicircle
